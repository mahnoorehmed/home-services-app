Steps to share this with your friends:
1. Open up the "Git" tab in Android Studio (or click the green checkmark ✔️ icon top right).
2. Expand the "Unversioned Files" or "Changes" list on the left.
3. Check the little box next to `HelloTeam.txt`.
4. Type a short message below like "Linked Antigravity to the final app folder".
5. Click "Commit and Push" at the bottom right.

Once you push it, have your friends click the blue "Pull" down arrow in their Android Studio!
